Description¶

The TMP103 is a digital output temperature sensor in a four-ball wafer chip-scale package (WCSP). The TMP103 is capable of reading temperatures to a resolution of 1°C. The TMP103 is specified for operation over a temperature range of -40°C to +125°C.

Resolution: 8 Bits Accuracy: ±1°C Typ (-10°C to +100°C)

The driver provides the common sysfs-interface for temperatures (see Naming and data format standards for sysfs files under Temperatures).

Please refer how to instantiate this driver: How to instantiate I2C devices
